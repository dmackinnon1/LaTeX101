Learning Goals

The learning goals for this module include:

  • Appreciate how LaTeX helps us communicate with math in digital environments.
  • Understand some of the collateral learning that happens when we learn how to use LaTeX.
  • Learn about some of the communities that support LaTeX and LaTeX users.

Before You Start

LaTeX as a Digital Literacy

What LaTeX Teaches

- as we strive to write clearer mathematics, we learn more about the notation and conventions of mathematics, and how mathematicians communicate and structure how they present mathematics. - as we learn LaTeX commands, we are exposed to a simple form of programming, one that resembles other markup and presentation languages, and is essential to digital communication. - as we work with electronic documents, wikis, and cloud-based systems, we learn more about how the internet and digital environments work.
